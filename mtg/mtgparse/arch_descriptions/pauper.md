# Pauper Deck Archetypes

## Mono Red Madness (10.3%)

**Key Cards:** Kessig Flamebreather, Lightning Bolt, Grab the Prize, Faithless Looting

Aggro/burn deck that uses discard outlets to cast madness spells at reduced cost while creatures like Kessig Flamebreather deal damage whenever a card is discarded, creating a synergistic engine that burns the opponent out quickly.

---

## Blue Terror (10.2%)

**Key Cards:** Tolarian Terror, Mental Note, Snuff Out, Lorien Revealed

Tempo deck that mills its own graveyard to cheaply cast Tolarian Terror as an efficient threat, backed by counterspells and card selection to protect it and maintain an advantage.

---

## Elves (7.3%)

**Key Cards:** Priest of Titania, Timberwatch Elf, Llanowar Elves, Lead the Stampede

Mono-green creature swarm deck that generates explosive mana through elf synergies and floods the board with creatures, using Timberwatch Elf to pump the team for lethal attacks.

---

## Grixis Affinity (6.5%)

**Key Cards:** Galvanic Blast, Thoughtcast, Reckoner's Bargain, Myr Enforcer

Artifact-based aggro deck that exploits free/cheap artifact creatures and uses Thoughtcast and Reckoner's Bargain for artifact-fueled card draw, with Galvanic Blast as efficient metalcraft removal.

---

## Jund Wildfire (5.7%)

**Key Cards:** Cleansing Wildfire, Cast Down, Ichor Wellspring, Writhing Chrysalis

Land destruction deck that uses Cleansing Wildfire to destroy the opponent's nonbasic lands while drawing cards, supported by Ichor Wellspring for additional card advantage and Cast Down for creature removal.

---

## Madness Burn (5.5%)

**Key Cards:** Alms of the Vein, Lightning Bolt, Vampire's Kiss, Faithless Looting

Rakdos (black/red) madness deck that uses discard outlets to cast Alms of the Vein at its reduced madness cost, combining repeated life drain with direct damage spells to burn opponents out quickly.

---

## White Aggro (5.1%)

**Key Cards:** Prismatic Strands, Kor Skyfisher, Thraben Charm, Thraben Inspector

White tempo/aggro deck that generates incremental card advantage through Thraben Inspector and bounces permanents with Kor Skyfisher, using Prismatic Strands (with flashback) as a combat trick to protect its board and swing past blockers.

---

## Mono Red Rally (5.0%)

**Key Cards:** Burning-Tree Emissary, Goblin Bushwhacker, Rally at the Hornburg, Galvanic Blast

Go-wide aggro deck that chains Burning-Tree Emissary for free creatures and then deploys Goblin Bushwhacker or Rally at the Hornburg to give the entire team haste and a power boost for a single explosive alpha strike.

---

## Caw-Gates (4.3%)

**Key Cards:** Squadron Hawk, Prismatic Strands, Lorien Revealed, Basilisk Gate

Control deck that uses Squadron Hawk to fill the hand with cheap flying threats and Basilisk Gate to pump one of them into a massive attacker, protected by Prismatic Strands and backed by blue card draw.

---

## Spy Combo (4.1%)

**Key Cards:** Balustrade Spy, Land Grant, Lotus Petal, Winding Way

Combo deck that runs zero basic lands, using Land Grant and Lotus Petal for fast mana and setup, then casts Balustrade Spy targeting itself to mill the entire library, winning by leveraging the stocked graveyard with recursive green threats.

---

## Dimir Faeries (3.1%)

**Key Cards:** Spellstutter Sprite, Snuff Out, Lorien Revealed, Ninja of the Deep Hours

Tempo deck that uses cheap faerie creatures to counter spells with Spellstutter Sprite and draw cards with Ninja of the Deep Hours, with Snuff Out handling threats that can't be countered.

---

## Tron (3.1%)

**Key Cards:** Expedition Map, Myr Retriever, Candy Trail, Urza's Power Plant

Ramp deck that assembles the three Urza lands to generate massive amounts of mana, tutoring pieces with Expedition Map and generating value with Myr Retriever's recursion and Candy Trail's card draw.

---

## Golgari Gardens (2.9%)

**Key Cards:** Avenging Hunter, Fanatical Offering, Defile, Khalni Garden

Sacrifice-based value deck that uses Khalni Garden to generate free 0/1 Plant tokens each turn, sacrifices them to Fanatical Offering for card draw, uses Defile as scalable removal, and deploys Avenging Hunter as a threatening creature with built-in poison synergy.

---

## Mono-Blue Faeries (2.3%)

**Key Cards:** Spellstutter Sprite, Snap, Counterspell, Ninja of the Deep Hours

Tempo deck that bounces Faerie Miscreant with Ninja of the Deep Hours for repeated card draw, counters spells with Spellstutter Sprite, and uses Snap as a tempo bounce spell that untaps lands to keep mana up.

---

## Gruul Ramp (2.1%)

**Key Cards:** Utopia Sprawl, Annoyed Altisaur, Avenging Hunter, Arbor Elf

Ramp deck that accelerates mana quickly via Arbor Elf untapping a Utopia Sprawl land, then casts large threats like Annoyed Altisaur and Avenging Hunter several turns ahead of schedule.

---

## Black Sacrifice (1.8%)

**Key Cards:** Carrion Feeder, Shambling Ghast, Mortician Beetle, Defile

Sacrifice-value deck that uses Shambling Ghast (makes a Treasure when it dies) and other expendable creatures as fodder, growing Carrion Feeder and Mortician Beetle through repeated sacrifices while Defile sweeps the opponent's board.

---

## Bogles (1.7%)

**Key Cards:** Slippery Bogle, Utopia Sprawl, Armadillo Cloak, Gladecover Scout

Hexproof aura deck that ramps with Utopia Sprawl to rapidly stack enchantments on untargetable creatures, with Armadillo Cloak providing trample and life gain and Slippery Bogle/Gladecover Scout as the protected threats.

---

## Dimir Terror (1.6%)

**Key Cards:** Tolarian Terror, Mental Note, Snuff Out, Lorien Revealed

Tempo deck that combines Blue Terror's graveyard-filling strategy with black's access to Snuff Out for efficient removal and Gurmag Angler as a second large threat.

---

## Gruul Ponza (1.4%)

**Key Cards:** Mwonvuli Acid-Moss, Thermokarst, Utopia Sprawl, Arbor Elf

Land destruction deck that uses fast mana from Arbor Elf and Utopia Sprawl to cast Mwonvuli Acid-Moss and Thermokarst on turns 2-3, permanently stunting the opponent's development while also searching for forests to keep its own mana flowing.

---

## Familiars (1.3%)

**Key Cards:** Sunscape Familiar, Snap, Cloud of Faeries, Archaeomancer

Combo deck that reduces spell costs with Sunscape/Nightscape Familiar, generates mana with Snap's land-untap effect and Cloud of Faeries, and wins by looping Ghostly Flicker with Archaeomancer for infinite mana and a lethal Rolling Thunder.

---

## Ephemerate Tron (1.2%)

**Key Cards:** Urza's Power Plant, Prophetic Prism, Ephemerate, Stonehorn Dignitary

Tron variant that uses the Urza lands for mana and Prophetic Prism for color fixing and card draw, locking out all attacks by repeatedly blinking Stonehorn Dignitary with Ephemerate, recovered via Mnemonic Wall.

---

## Dredge (1.2%)

**Key Cards:** Stinkweed Imp, Generous Ent, Malevolent Rumble, Faithless Looting

Graveyard deck that uses cards with the dredge mechanic to replace draws and rapidly fill the graveyard, generating advantage through Stinkweed Imp's recursive blocking and Malevolent Rumble for digging to key permanents.

---

## Glintblade (0.8%)

**Key Cards:** Glint Hawk, Kor Skyfisher, Tithing Blade, Refurbished Familiar

White/black artifact value deck that uses Glint Hawk and Kor Skyfisher to repeatedly bounce and replay cheap artifacts like Tithing Blade and Refurbished Familiar, generating incremental card advantage and draining life.

---

## Walls Combo (0.8%)

**Key Cards:** Axebane Guardian, Quirion Ranger, Winding Way, Freed from the Real

Combo deck that taps Axebane Guardian for large amounts of mana equal to the number of defenders, uses Quirion Ranger for untap tricks and Freed from the Real to go infinite, then casts a large mana sink to win.

---

## Food Gardens (0.8%)

**Key Cards:** Lembas, Fanatical Offering, Khalni Garden, Cast Down

Gardens variant that generates both food tokens (Lembas) and Plant creature tokens (Khalni Garden) to sacrifice to Fanatical Offering for repeated card draw, backed by Cast Down for removal.

---

## Jeskai Ephemerate (0.6%)

**Key Cards:** Ephemerate, Counterspell, Lorien Revealed, Mnemonic Wall

Value-oriented blink deck that repeatedly enters ETB creatures with Ephemerate, recurs the spell with Mnemonic Wall, and supplements with Counterspell and blue card draw to maintain control of the game.

---

## Turbo Fog (0.6%)

**Key Cards:** Moment's Peace, Lembas, Fanatical Offering, Khalni Garden

Control deck that repeatedly prevents all damage with Moment's Peace (flashback) and sustains itself through a food/token draw engine — sacrificing Khalni Garden's Plant tokens to Fanatical Offering — to keep drawing fogs and eventually exhaust the opponent.

---

## Izzet Terror (0.6%)

**Key Cards:** Tolarian Terror, Skred, Counterspell, Lorien Revealed

Tempo deck that fills the graveyard to cast Tolarian Terror cheaply, using snow-based Skred as the primary removal spell and Counterspell for protection.

---

## Esper Affinity (0.5%)

**Key Cards:** Thoughtcast, Glint Hawk, Myr Enforcer, Kor Skyfisher

White/blue/black affinity deck that layers the artifact aggro core with Thoughtcast for free card draw and bounce creatures for repeated ETB value, augmented by black removal.

---

## Kiln Fiend (0.5%)

**Key Cards:** Kiln Fiend, Rite of Flame, Manamorphose, Temur Battle Rage

Combo-aggro deck that chains rituals (Rite of Flame) and free spells (Manamorphose) to pump Kiln Fiend (+3/+0 per instant/sorcery), then uses Temur Battle Rage for double strike and trample to kill in a single attack.

---

## Poison Storm (0.5%)

**Key Cards:** Contentious Plan, Infectious Inquiry, Manamorphose, Vivisurgeon's Insight

Storm-style combo deck that chains proliferate spells to accumulate poison counters on the opponent, using Manamorphose for free storm count and Weather the Storm for life insurance while assembling enough proliferate triggers for lethal.

---

## Boros Bully (0.5%)

**Key Cards:** Kor Skyfisher, Faithless Looting, Lightning Bolt, Rally the Peasants

White/red tempo deck that uses Faithless Looting for card selection and Kor Skyfisher for bounce value, threatening a lethal team attack with Rally the Peasants cast twice (main phase and flashback).

---

## Jund Gardens (0.4%)

**Key Cards:** Fanatical Offering, Nihil Spellbomb, Skred, Khalni Garden

Jund-colored Gardens variant that adds red for Skred (snow-based removal) and Nihil Spellbomb as an artifact-sacrifice piece with graveyard hate, fueling the Fanatical Offering draw engine.

---

## Izzet Faeries (0.4%)

**Key Cards:** Spellstutter Sprite, Counterspell, Lorien Revealed, Lightning Bolt

Faerie tempo variant that supplements the blue faerie core with red's Lightning Bolt for more ways to answer threats that can't be countered.

---

## Boros Moxite (0.4%)

**Key Cards:** Melded Moxite, Glint Hawk, Galvanic Blast, Lightning Bolt

Artifact aggro deck centered on Melded Moxite, using Glint Hawk to bounce and replay it for value, backed by efficient Boros burn (Galvanic Blast, Lightning Bolt) to clear the path for aggressive attacks.

---

## Selesnya Ramp (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Utopia Sprawl, Avenging Hunter, Generous Ent, Arbor Elf

Green/white ramp deck that uses the Arbor Elf + Utopia Sprawl engine to power out large threats like Avenging Hunter and Generous Ent far ahead of curve, supplemented by white's removal and lifegain.

---

## Mono-White Heroic (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Ethereal Armor, Hyena Umbra, Sentinel's Eyes, Lagonna-Band Trailblazer

Combo-aggro deck that targets its own heroic creatures with auras to trigger rapid growth, with Hyena Umbra providing totem armor insurance, Ethereal Armor for a massive power boost, and Sentinel's Eyes as a cheap recurring enchantment.

---

## Jeskai Synthesizer (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Glint Hawk, Experimental Synthesizer, Sacred Cat, Lightning Bolt

Artifact value deck that bounces Experimental Synthesizer with Glint Hawk for repeated card impulses, uses Sacred Cat as a resilient life-gaining threat, and clears blockers with Lightning Bolt.

---

## Burn (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Lightning Bolt, Galvanic Blast, Experimental Synthesizer, Rift Bolt

Burn deck that uses Experimental Synthesizer as a card advantage engine — exiling the top card and letting you play it immediately — to maintain a constant stream of damage spells backed by Galvanic Blast's metalcraft burst.

---

## Jeskai Affinity (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Utrom Monitor, Thoughtcast, Galvanic Blast, Glint Hawk

Three-color affinity variant built around Utrom Monitor as the key creature alongside free Thoughtcast draw and Galvanic Blast removal, using the artifact synergy package across white, blue, and red.

---

## Serpentine Curve (0.3%)

**Key Cards:** Serpentine Curve, Lorien Revealed, Counterspell, Pieces of the Puzzle

Spell-heavy combo-control deck that fills the graveyard with instants and sorceries to empower Serpentine Curve as a large threat, using Lorien Revealed and Pieces of the Puzzle for card selection and Counterspell for protection.

---

## Orzhov Ephemerate (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Kor Skyfisher, Thraben Charm, Refurbished Familiar, Ephemerate

White/black blink value deck that repeatedly bounces and replays Refurbished Familiar (an artifact creature that recurs from the graveyard) and other ETB creatures with Kor Skyfisher and Ephemerate to grind out value.

---

## Cycle Storm (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Dark Ritual, Cabal Ritual, Songs of the Damned, Manamorphose

Black storm combo deck that cycles creatures into the graveyard to fuel Songs of the Damned (adds B for each creature in the graveyard), chains Dark Ritual and Cabal Ritual for explosive mana, and wins with a Tendrils of Agony storm kill.

---

## Devotion (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Gray Merchant of Asphodel, Chittering Rats, Refurbished Familiar, Defile

Mono-Black Devotion deck that accumulates black permanents to maximize Gray Merchant of Asphodel's life-drain trigger, disrupting the opponent's hand with Chittering Rats and using Refurbished Familiar for recurring artifact value.

---

## Dimir Control (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Tolarian Terror, Snuff Out, Lorien Revealed, Counterspell

Full control deck that answers threats with Snuff Out and Counterspell, uses Lorien Revealed for card advantage, and closes the game with a cheaply-cast Tolarian Terror.

---

## Ruby Storm (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Seething Song, Rite of Flame, Manamorphose, Grapeshot

Storm combo deck that chains Seething Song, Rite of Flame, and Manamorphose to rapidly build a high storm count and kills with Grapeshot or an army from Empty the Warrens.

---

## Grixis Control (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Snuff Out, Lorien Revealed, Ponder, Gurmag Angler

Three-color control deck that uses Ponder and Lorien Revealed for card selection, Snuff Out for efficient removal, and closes the game with an efficiently costed Gurmag Angler.

---

## Esper Ephemerate (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Ephemerate, Augur of Bolas, Lorien Revealed, Mnemonic Wall

White/blue/black blink control deck that uses Ephemerate on Augur of Bolas for repeated instant/sorcery tutoring and Mnemonic Wall to recur Ephemerate, locking out the opponent while generating sustained card advantage.

---

## Mono Red Pingers (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Kessig Flamebreather, Wrenn's Resolve, Galvanic Blast, Faithless Looting

Red deck that uses Kessig Flamebreather as the primary damage source — triggering its "deal 1 damage when you discard" ability repeatedly through draw-discard spells like Wrenn's Resolve and Faithless Looting — backed by Galvanic Blast for artifact-fueled removal.

---

## Goblins (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Goblin Matron, Exhume, Fanatical Offering, Mogg War Marshal

Tribal goblin deck with a reanimator twist — Goblin Matron tutors key goblins, Exhume quickly reanimates them from the graveyard, and Fanatical Offering provides a sacrifice outlet while Mogg War Marshal floods the board with tokens.

---

## Naya Ephemerate (0.2%)

**Key Cards:** Writhing Chrysalis, Sacred Cat, Lightning Bolt, Ephemerate

Three-color (white/green/red) blink value deck that uses Ephemerate on ETB creatures like Writhing Chrysalis and Sacred Cat for repeated triggers, with Lightning Bolt for removal.

---

## Mono-Black (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Snuff Out, Defile, Gray Merchant of Asphodel, Chittering Rats

Midrange/control deck that disrupts the opponent's hand with Chittering Rats, controls the board with Snuff Out and Defile, and closes out with repeated Gray Merchant of Asphodel life-drain triggers.

---

## Rakdos Affinity (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Galvanic Blast, Fanatical Offering, Reckoner's Bargain, Myr Enforcer

Red/black affinity variant that supplements the free artifact threat package with Fanatical Offering and Reckoner's Bargain as sacrifice-based card draw, creating a more value-oriented version of the affinity strategy.

---

## Dimir Delver (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Exhume, Ponder, Counterspell, Delver of Secrets

Reanimator-tempo hybrid that uses Ponder to set up both Delver of Secrets flips and fast Exhume plays, reanimating large threats like Gurmag Angler as early as turn 2 while Counterspell protects the gameplan.

---

## Azorius Affinity (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Utrom Monitor, Thoughtcast, Springleaf Drum, Glint Hawk

White/blue affinity variant built around Utrom Monitor and Springleaf Drum for mana generation, with Thoughtcast for free card draw and Glint Hawk for artifact bounce value.

---

## Mono-Green Aggro (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Writhing Chrysalis, Witch's Mark, Nest Invader, Nettle Sentinel

Fast green stompy deck that curves out with efficient creatures like Writhing Chrysalis and Nest Invader (which provides a mana-producing Eldrazi Spawn), using Witch's Mark as an aura for additional pressure.

---

## 4c Ephemerate (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Writhing Chrysalis, Sacred Cat, Thraben Charm, Ephemerate

Four-color blink value deck that uses Ephemerate on ETB-powerful creatures like Writhing Chrysalis and Sacred Cat across multiple colors, gaining access to the best blink targets in the format.

---

## Azorius Faeries (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Spellstutter Sprite, Counterspell, Lorien Revealed, Journey to Nowhere

Faerie tempo variant that adds white's Journey to Nowhere to handle ground threats that blue can't counter or bounce, rounding out the interactive package.

---

## Mardu Synthesizer (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Glint Hawk, Galvanic Blast, Lembas, Experimental Synthesizer

White/black/red artifact value deck using Glint Hawk to bounce Lembas and Experimental Synthesizer for repeated card advantage, backed by Galvanic Blast and broader Mardu removal options.

---

## Storm (0.1%)

**Key Cards:** Dark Ritual, Exhume, Not Dead After All, Songs of the Damned

Black reanimator-storm combo deck that uses Dark Ritual and Songs of the Damned for explosive mana and Exhume or Not Dead After All to rapidly put a large creature into play from the graveyard as early as turn 1.

---
